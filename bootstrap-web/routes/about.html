About me

What do you want to know..?

Laurence is a guitarist, composer, author, editor and publisher. He has music degree, Grade 8 in classical guitar, Grade 8 in music theory and an ABRSM instrumental teaching diploma. Laurence has written and/or edited several popular books on guitar playing, and also served on the advisory panel for an international acoustic guitar examination syllabus. At Guitar Command, Laurence handles article writing, music typesetting and the business side of things. Laurence specializes in classical, jazz and acoustic guitar playing.
